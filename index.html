Who Are We?
Scholarships?

When Project Catalyst began its initial funding round, it had 42 NFT based proposals. Our core founders decided that if these project proposers came together to build out our shared NFT visions that it would be more beneficial to the Cardano community than to compete with one another, we shared that vision with our community. NFT-DAO has since onboarded many would-be competitors, and has expanded its vision to onboard university students so that we can mentor and educate the future generation of blockchain innovators.

Students who join NFT-DOA through the universities have the potential to earn scholarships. These scholarships are funded with ADA (Cardano's native currency) generated by our projects and is part of what sets us apart from other DAO’s. This allows us to scout out new talents and mentor new generations. Many of our members have had their projects featured at Cardano hosted events and have been creating new infrastructure on the Cardano blockchain, for more information on our team members accomplishments outside of the DAO see below.

Student Blockchain Initiative
What makes us Unique?

Many of our DAO members have a background in or are currently working in higher education. All members felt it would be beneficial to the DAO, universities, and Cardano community to give students hands-on experience in the world of blockchain. We have been progressively building our relationship with the: Blockchain Innovation Center at the University of Wyoming…Initial funded by Charles Hoskinson University of Arkansas University of North Dakota

NFT- DAO is unique because of our Student Blockchain initiative and the mentorship program.

OUR CURRENT GOAL
HOW ARE WE FUNDED?

We are actively building a framework called “Box-Car”, this framework will allow individuals of any technical level to build an NFT marketplace on the Cardano blockchain. This framework will work similar to Shopify's(link to a shopify youtube video/ we will make one ourselves at some point) plug and play web page builders and backend applications.

Well, we’re funded by the Cardano community ♥ NFT-DAO’s projects are funded through IOG’s Project Catalyst initiative Want to learn more about what our funds are being used for and how we plan to use future revenue for scholarships? Head to our about us page (link) to get to know us better.

HOW ARE WE ACCOMPLISHING IT?

To accomplish this goal we are building out the necessary applications for the Box-Car first. This includes payment infrastructure to accept crypto currency, blockchain domain hosting, on demand NFT minting infrastructure, and much more!
